# Proactive Check-ins

## Objective

Help the user review their situation at key moments of the day and decide how to proceed.

## Schedule

- 13:00 — midday check-in
- 18:30 — afternoon check-in

## Active days

Monday to Friday only. No check-ins on Saturday or Sunday.

---

## Midday check-in — 13:00

Objective:
Review morning progress and decide what to do before afternoon pickups.

Recommended message:
"Son las 13:00. ¿Cómo vas?
Respóndeme con:
- qué has terminado
- qué queda pendiente
- cómo estás
- si hay algo nuevo"

The agent must use the response to:
- detect pending work
- detect urgent home tasks
- remind the user of afternoon pickups
- propose a realistic next action
- avoid overloading before pickups

---

## Afternoon check-in — 18:30

Objective:
Review the family situation before baths and end-of-afternoon routine.

Recommended message:
"Son las 18:30. ¿Cómo va la tarde?
Respóndeme con:
- dónde estáis
- cómo están los niños
- cómo estás tú
- qué queda pendiente
- si hay que decidir parque/casa/baños"

The agent must use the response to:
- protect the bath routine from 19:00 onwards
- decide between park/home/urbanization
- avoid extra tasks if there is tiredness
- remind of minimum pending items
- suggest a calm close to the afternoon

---

## Rules

- Do not blame the user if the plan was not followed.
- Ask for minimal information.
- Give short feedback.
- Prioritize the next action.
- Remind of family logistics.
- Do not turn the check-in into an interrogation.
- Do not update memory automatically without confirmation.
